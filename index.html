Salon Organizer App

User Appointment Service Staff Customer Payment Product Inventory Review Schedule Promotion Location

User

Attributes: User ID, Name, Contact Information, Address, Role, Profile Picture

Relationships: Customers can book appointments. Staff can manage schedules.

Appointment

Attributes: Appointment ID, Customer ID, Staff ID, Service ID, Appointment Date and Time, Status, Notes

Relationships: Linked to Users (Customers and Staff). Linked to specific Services.

Service

Attributes: Service ID, Name, Duration, Price, Description, Category

Relationships: Can be booked in an Appointment.

Staff

Attributes: Staff ID, Name, Contact Information, Role/Position, Availability, Specialization

Relationships: Assigned to Appointments.

Customer

Attributes: Customer ID, Name, Contact Information, Preferences, Booking History, Membership or Loyalty Status

Relationships: Can book multiple Appointments.

Payment

Attributes: Payment ID, Appointment ID, Amount Paid, Payment Method, Date and Time, Status

Relationships: Linked to Appointments.

Product

Attributes: Product ID, Name, Price, Stock Quantity, Description

Relationships: Can be added to a sale or linked to Services.

Inventory

Attributes: Inventory ID, Product ID, Quantity, Reorder Threshold

Relationships: Tracks Products used or sold in the salon.

Review

Attributes: Review ID, Customer ID, Staff ID or Service ID, Rating, Comments, Date

Relationships: Linked to Customers, Staff, or Services.

Schedule

Attributes: Schedule ID, Staff ID, Date and Time Slots, Availability Status

Relationships: Linked to Staff and Appointments.

Promotion/Offer

Attributes: Promotion ID, Name, Description, Discount Amount/Percentage, Validity Period, Eligible Services or Products

Relationships: Linked to Services, Products, or Customers.

Salon Location

Attributes: Location ID, Address, Contact Information, Working Hours

Relationships: Linked to Appointments and Staff.
